Seneng Utami


I graduated from senior high school in 2009. Around 8 years of my life I have been working as a helper, working for Chinese families in Indonesia, Singapore and Hong Kong.

While being a helper, I faced some kind of discrimination, felt isolated, and often got some verbal abuse from my employers. Those really made my brain unable to think properly and having difficulties with concentration.

Within a few months, right after I came back to Indonesia, I started to learn coding from scratch. I learn HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, some JavaScript, Ruby and C. With my lack of understanding English, the way I learn to code became harder than to understand the English language itself. So, the way I learned to code it was quite slow. How slow it is, I didn't give up and just kept learning.

When working as a helper, I felt anxious and paranoid a lot. Even now it takes some time to calm my mind from any anxieties when it comes and goes. It was hard to stay focused and had difficulty understanding what I read. I found an article about programming as a good way of Cognitive Behavioural Therapy for mental health, it gave me more strength to learn coding. I also believe that in the long term, I intend to provide programming courses for free to help other people who have problems with mental health issues especially for women (such as former domestic workers, girls who weren't able to go school, or anyone who is interested to keep learning). This is one of the articles that I found: How Learning Programming Can Help Your Mental Health. Once I started learning coding, right now I can feel the difference in my mind for thinking a little bit more properly.

With all the difficulties I had in my experiences while learning coding(having imposter syndrome), I would like to build a project to support and give access to young people without computer science degrees who are interested to learn programming, or computer science students who have financial dificulties, I want to help them by improve their learning skills in programming base on their capabilities and directly bring them to connect with professional developers.

My career goal is to be a software developer. I want to put both my photography creativity and my coding skills into websites. Currently I live in Nepal and I network with the software development company and the developers here.

I have a chance to see the development work. I was amazed to see directly how machine learning can detect something, how the website is built and these things really are useful in our daily life. Programming/ coding is part of Technology, I want to be able to be part of people who are always hungry for innovation. To change life for the better. Regardless of who we are, and where we are from.

I am also working on a website to showcase my photography. I built the website by myself while learning programming and I want others to be able to do similar works as well. The website is continuously being updated.
